Course Information

How this course will be organized:

The course will be separated into modules depending on the section of DaVinci Resolve, regardless of which operating system or computer is being used to run DaVinci Resolve for editing creative projects. It will also include different notes on additional steps in the versions of the course for additional steps for different parts of DaVinci Resolve whenever necessary, depending on the devices operating system and hardware requirements. There will be different recommendations for different features on DaVinci Resolve on the hardware side that can impact the experience on DaVinci Resolve that will be mentioned.

Pricing:

There is a bundle of the course which has a higher price but is intended to be the combination of the 2 main individual versions for the operating systems that will be used for this course. The 2 main individual operating systems, however, are the same price to keep it fair between them. This is because usually some operating systems have a fee associated with the different price change for the manufacturer to get a slice of the pie, so to speak, for the cut of the revenue. I decided against doing this because I don’t feel it would be fair for the customer. That is why this is priced the way it is.
